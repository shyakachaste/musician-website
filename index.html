About

Fred Robinson Mugisha ( Element )

is a producer , musician and songwriter who is known in producing songs for many artists including Bruce Melody, Mani Martin, The Ben, Meddy and international artists such as WizKid, Davido and many others.He is best known for his special tag 'Eleeeh', at the beginning or end of all his works.

He has produced many of today's most popular songs such as; Fou Toi by Element ft (Ross kana and Bruce melodie), Zanzibar by Bruce melodie ft Harmonize, , Bermuda by Davisd ft Bull dog and Bushali, Pasadena by Christopher, Kashe by Element, Cakula by Dj phil peter ft Drama-T and many more.Later It's been less than 4 years since he started making songs for artists, and he is currently working with great artists.
